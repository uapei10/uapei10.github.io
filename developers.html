Functionalities and objectives

Data

Data management and processing collected by radars and cameras. Graphics creation to represent information.

Recognition

Object recognition and division by subtypes, more specifically, people, bicycles/motorcycles, cars or trucks with the help of YOLO, a image recognition tool.

Demonstration of information from panels:

Collection and storage of data.

Data processing.

Test panel using Graph.js.

Usability tests.

Integration of all requirements.

Sensory Fusion:

Increased success rate of object identification.

Collection of data from the two sensors.

Processing of the obtained data.

Storage of data obtained after the merge.
