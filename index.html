ABOUT OUR PROJECT

This project emerged from Universidade de Aveiro, on the initiative of PASMO (Plataforma Aberta para o desenvolvimento e experimentação de Soluções para a Mobilidade).
PASMO has a set of equipment installed on the beaches of Costa Nova and Barra, as well as on A25, which aims to classify and quantify vehicles.
Our goal is to provide a platform that displays the stored data in relevant ways, in order to further improve traffic management, while being useful to the population.
We support mobility and road safety!
